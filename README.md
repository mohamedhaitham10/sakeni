# Sakeni Business Plan Package
**March 2025**

Welcome to the complete Sakeni Business Plan package! I've taken the core business model from your original Arabic documents (the Fluter/Firebase stack, EGP 100K capital, no-broker-fee strategy) and organized it into these 9 professional, investor-ready deliverables.

## Included Files (Ready to Use)
1. **idea summary.docx** 
   * A comprehensive breakdown of the core problem, solution, revenue model, and market size based on the template.

2. **FI Market Research Template.xlsx** 
   * 4 sheets covering market demographics, competitor analysis, targeted survey data (students vs landlords), and basic financial benchmarks.

3. **compitive analysis.png** 
   * A high-resolution, professional visual matrix comparing Sakeni to OLX, Facebook Groups, and traditional brokers.

4. **product development roadmap.xlsx** 
   * A 3-phase Gantt chart covering all technical, marketing, and operational milestones over a 4-year period.

5. **FI sample specification.docx** 
   * A deep-dive technical specification covering the Flutter & Firebase architecture, data models, and feature rollout plans.

6. **executive summary.docx** 
   * A 2-page concise overview tailored for potential investors or partners outlining the EGP 100K ask and the 6-month launch plan.

7. **Status and Plan Dummy BPlan .xlsx** 
   * A dashboard and implementation tracker that lists the exact tasks (planning, design, tech, ops, legal) and their budgets.

8. **Sample biz Plan.pdf**
   * A comprehensive, beautifully formatted PDF version of the full business plan, combining the executive summary, problem/solution, revenue model, go-to-market strategy, and team vision.

9. **9 - Sakeni Pitch Deck.pptx** (NEW BONUS)
   * A 9-slide presentation deck ready to be used in meetings, covering the problem, the solution, the market, the revenue model, and the financial ask.
